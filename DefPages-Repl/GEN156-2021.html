The Hebrew University and The Lady Davis Fellowship Trust
For Post-Doctoral Researchers - 2021/22

 
*PLEASE NOTE:  One of the main aims of setting up an online system for applications is that each candidate can check that all the information he/she submitted has been uploaded.  We will not check the status of candidates and their application files.  IT IS THE CANDIDATES’ RESPONSIBILITY TO DO SO.
 
 

How to Apply:

Please ensure that you fulfill the minimum criteria for eligibility for a Post-Doctoral Fellowship as described HERE

If you do not satisfy the criteria the Trust will not be able to process your application.

 

Last date to submit applications: December 31st, 2020

 If you want to be safe that your file is complete, get your material in by the 30th of December…don't wait until the last minute.  We are not responsible for documents that didn't make it into your file after the deadline.

Submission Procedure

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Upload the following documents (in English OR Hebrew) according to the upload instructions:

  • Personal Letter - optional

  • Curriculum Vitae (Please do not include List of Publications in your CV)

  • A List of your Major Publications

  • Research Proposal (no more than 2 pages)

  • A letter from your Main Sponsor (your sponsor may not be one of your references too): Expressing the interest of the Faculty/Department at the Hebrew University in the research you are pursuing - The academic main sponsors should transfer their letter/s directly to the applicant, the applicant should upload the letter to the system independently.

  • A Letter from Secondary Sponsors (optional): Expressing further support for your application - The academic secondary sponsors should transfer their letter/s directly to the applicant, the applicant should upload the letter to the system independently.

  • A health certificate (In ENGLISH OR HEBREW ONLY) from your doctor stating that you are in general good health and are able to work (NO OTHER TESTS ARE NECESSARY).  This document is a required document and failure to supply it will hinder processing your application.

*Please do not upload abstracts or any other extra information we have not asked for, any additional information will be discarded from your application.

C. At least two letters of reference (but NOT MORE than 3, please note that If you receive more than 3 letters we will discard the extra letters randomly) - sent according to the guidelines des​cribed here

D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
