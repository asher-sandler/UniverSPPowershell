Tenure Track Positions at ELSC - 2021-2022

 

Application Deadline: September 24, 2021 

 

Tenure-track positions are now open at the Edmond and Lily Safra Center for Brain Sciences (ELSC) of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. We are looking for excellent early-career scientists with a strong interest in establishing active innovative and interdisciplinary research programs in neuroscience.

We at ELSC provide a thriving interface between theoretical and experimental neuroscience across all levels, and build on the Hebrew University's record of excellence and innovation in our multidisciplinary approach to brain science. 

We offer:

  • State-of-the-art research facilities

  • State-of-the-art custom-built laboratories

  • A top-ranking, interdisciplinary Ph.D. program

  • An outstanding academic milieu 

  • Competitive start-up funding

 

This position is open to scientists specializing in the following areas:

  1. Theoretical and computational neuroscience

  2. Cognitive neuroscience

  3. System neuroscience

  4. Molecular neuroscience 

 

We encourage theoretically-oriented scientists to apply.

HUJI supports greater equality, stronger diversity,
and the increased representation of women in the sciences.

Application Submission

A. A CV Form  (see CV instructions)

B. A scientific biography with the following documents, uploaded according to the upload instructions:  

  • A letter of intent (maximum - 1 page)

  • An outline of future research and scientific vision (maximum 3 pages)

  • A list of publications (see format guidelines)

  • Copies of 3 representative publications (as PDFs)

C. Letters of recommendation from the Ph.D. advisor and the postdoctoral host (sent directly to ELSC according to the  instructions).  

D. Please complete by following the instructions on the 'Submission Status' page

 

 Application Deadline: September 17, 2021 

 

Important dates

On November 22-24, 2021, select candidates will present their work in person to the neuroscience community of the Hebrew University and meet with members of the recruitment committee.

On December 20, 2021, select candidates will present their work via Zoom to the ELSC international steering committee.

 

 

For more information, please contact us at brain@elsc.huji.ac.il

or by phone at  +972-2-658-5927
