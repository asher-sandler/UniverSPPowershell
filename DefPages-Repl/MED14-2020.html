WELCOME TO THE FACULTY OF MEDICINE'S STUDENTS EXCHANGE PROGRAM WEBPAGE

 

You have been chosen as one of the top 36 candidates in your track to be interviewed for the Faculty of Medicine's prestigious Students Exchange Program. The interviews for this program will be conducted in English and will determine who will be selected for the 10-20 slots available for the Regular track and Tzameret track. The interviews (two dates for each track) will take place in January and February 2022.

IMPORTANT: If you do not wish to participate in the Exchange program, please inform Elisheva Dovev at elishevad@savion.huji.ac.il as soon as possible so that other people may enjoy this opportunity!

If you are selected for the program, you will be given the opportunity to spend a period of time in your sixth year in a medical facility abroad. The programs are for the entire period only.

Please note that due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the number of of medical institutions abroad willing to host foreign students is still uncertain. Therfore we cannot yet determine the final number of students who will be able to participate in the exchange program.

 

 

Application Submission

A. Please fill out the  Online Application Form
 
B. Upload the following document via the Documents Upload page:

  • About myself in Word format, up to 200 words, in English - including:

    • Name and ID number at the top of the page

    • A photo

    • Special interests and future directions in medicine

    • Expectations from the Students' Exchange Program

    • Personal hobbies and fields of interest
C. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
 

YOUR APPLICATION FORM AND 'ABOUT MYSELF' DOCUMENT MUST BE UPLOADED

BY JANUARY 12, 2022

 

  1. You do not need to choose a specific university until the above deadline but when it is time to do so choose your destination according to what the each specific university offers and how its program suits you: Financially, academically, family wise and time wise.

  2. You may not approach the participating universities directly for special requests. There is no additional financial help from our faculty or from the inviting faculty other then what the various programs offer.
