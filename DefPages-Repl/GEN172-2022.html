The Kaye Innovation Awards at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem 2022

The Authority for Research and Development is pleased to invite applications for the 2022 Kaye Innovation Awards.

All university faculty, staff and students are eligible to apply.  The awards are in recognition of the development of a new product or system that has been marketed, brought revenue or savings to the Hebrew University, or that has significant commercial potential.


Submission Date: March 1st, 2022

 

The prizes will be awarded in a ceremony at the University's Board of Governors Meeting to be held during the week of June 12-15, 2022. Awardees are required to attend the ceremony.

Submission Procedure:

A. Complete and save the online application form (personal information questionnaire)
B. Upload the following documents via the Document Upload page
  • A short CV of the candidate (including ID number).
  • A colored photo of the candidate (in jpeg format).
  • An abstract of the invention (up to 2 pages)
  • A detailed description of the invention's revenues, savings for the University or marketing potential
  • Articles written by the candidate (or figures, illustrations, etc.)
  • A colored picture of the invention (in jpeg format).

C. Two recommendations from University staff or from the relevant industry. (student candidates: one recommendation must be from the supervisor).  See instructions.

D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
