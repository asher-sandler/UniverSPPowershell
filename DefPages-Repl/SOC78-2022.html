Joint position in Psychology and Cognitive Science
Tenure Track Faculty Positions - 2022

 

Applications should arrive by September 30th, 2021

 

 

The Department of Psychology and the Department of Cognitive and Brain Sciences at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel are recruiting for an open rank joint tenure-track faculty position. We welcome all applicants, especially those whose research deals with one of these topics: (1) the integration of biology and psychology, (2) psychopathology (3) cognitive aspects of human development.  

The successful applicant will conduct research, teach both undergraduate (BA) and graduate (MA, PhD) courses in the departments, and supervise students' research. We welcome collaboration with other department members.

The Department of Psychology and the Department of Cognitive and Brain Sciences at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem are at the forefront of psychological and cognitive-neuroscientific research in Israel. The Hebrew University, with approximately 20,000 students, provides a strong foundation of resources to support the research programs of all faculty members. Both Departments are located at the Mount Scopus campus in Jerusalem. 

Our departments are committed to building a diverse workforce and student body, and modeling an inclusive community. We encourage applications that are in keeping with this commitment, from members of all sections of the community.

We expect the position to begin in the academic year of 2022-2023. Opening the position is subject to budgetary considerations.  

Applicants are asked to submit a letter detailing current research and teaching interests, a curriculum vitae, representative reprints or preprints of publications, and three letters of reference by September 30th, 2021.

For information about the Department of Psychology at The Hebrew University, visit https://en.psychology.huji.ac.il/. For information about the Department of Brain and Cognitive Sciences at The Hebrew University, visit https://en.cognitive.huji.ac.il.

For further information about the position, contact Prof. Ariel Knafo-Noam, Chair of the Department of Psychology (psych.chair@mail.huji.ac.il), or Prof. Hilla Jacobson, Chair of the Department of Cognitive Sciences (hilla.jacobson@mail.huji.ac.il).

 

Application Submission:

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Letter of application

  • Curriculum vitae

  • Statement of research

  • Statement of teaching interests

  • Up to 3 samples of representative reprints and preprints

C. Three letters of reference - sent according to the guidelines described here
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submissi​on Status' page
