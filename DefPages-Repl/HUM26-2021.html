The Department of Cognitive Sciences  

Invites outstanding candidates to apply for tenure-track positions


With preference for the following fields:

  • Biological Brain Sciences

  • Computational Modeling of Cognition


The rank of each appointment will be determined according to the candidate's qualifications.

Candidates are requested to apply in writing to: Professor Hilla Jacobson, Chair of the Department of Cognitive Sciences

 

Application Submission

 
A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Cover letter

  • Doctoral diploma or official confirmation of eligibility

  • Curriculum vitae (according to the instructions and the template attached, click here to download)

  • Current list of publications (according to the instructions and the template attached, click here to download)

  • Statement of research and teaching plans (up to three pages and according to the instructions, click here to download)

  • Teaching evaluations (from the past three years)

  • Two representative publications

C. Letters of recommendation by  two scholars - sent according to the guidelines described here

D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
 
Complete applications must be submitted by
January 9th, 2020
 

Applications submitted not in accordance with these guidelines will not be consider.     

For additional information, please contact the Cognitive Sciences Department Coordinator, Ms. Shani Cohen via email: shanic@savion.huji.ac.il

 

This announcement does not constitute a commitment to accept candidates in any or all fields.
