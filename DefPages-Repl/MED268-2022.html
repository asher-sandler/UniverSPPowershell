The Israeli Bioinnovators Fellowship & Mentorship by Teva

For Post docs and PhD students (following submission of a research proposal)

 

Application Deadline: November 15,2021​

  TEVA's Request for Applications

Application procedure

 

A. Fill out the online application form

B. Upload the following documents according to the following instructions:

Requirements for all application documents

PART I

Cover Page

PART II
Short description of the overall Research Project (up to 3 pages – including:)  

    • Research Project title
    • Summary of the Research Project (up to 300 words)
    • Scientific background including a short review of existing research in the field
    • Defined Research Project objectives and expected impact on the therapeutic field
    • Research Project description (including methodology)
    • Innovative aspects and Significance
    • Time table plan with main milestones and expected completion dates (up to 1 page)
    • Collaboration with other Institutions if relevant

 
PART III
Applicant Declaration: Applicants must declare that they will obtain all required approvals for conducting the research project from relevant authorities

 
PART IV
1. Short video (2 min) of the candidate explaining the motivation to join, presenting himself/herself and his/her research project

 
2. Curriculum Vitae of the Student (up to 2 pages):
    • Name (Title, Last, First, Initial)
    • Email
    • ID number
    • Affiliation: PI name, Institution and Department
    • Phone no (office and cell phone number)
    • Photo
    • Educational Background (years, institution, expertise, degree)
    • Relevant Employment History (years, institution, area of research, title)
    • List of Publications by Applicant
    • List of awards and/or scholarships received by applicant (year, title of  award, source)
3. Letter of endorsement from the student's PI - sent according to these instructions

 
4. Acknowledgement form: The Students and PIs are required to sign the Acknowledgement form attached as Appendix C of the RFA

 

C. To complete the process, please visit the Submission Status page and follow the instructions
