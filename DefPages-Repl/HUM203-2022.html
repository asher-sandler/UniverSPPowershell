Welcome - Application for the Academic Year 2024-25

 
We advise you to start the application process as early as possible.
You can manage and update your application until 19.09.2023.
Please make sure that your application is complete by this date: Note that the registration website will be closed at 22:00 local time!
 
Before uploading your application, read the full version of the Call
 

Application Submission

Please note: all documents including recommendations must be in English only!
A. Fill out the Online Fellowship Form

  • Please fill in all educational information fields and click 'Save'.

B. The following documents (only one file for each type of document) should be uploaded via the  Documents Upload page

  • A complete CV and List of Publications (List of Publications – Your list of publications may include up to twenty items. If your list is longer, please select the most important items)

  • A discursive description of the research project to be pursued in Jerusalem (up to five pages double-spaced). There is no need to include a list of bibliography.

  • A one-page abstract of the Ph.D. dissertation

C. Two letters of recommendation - sent according to the guidelines described here
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page

​The last date for applications is 19.09.2023
