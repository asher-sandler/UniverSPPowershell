The Institute of Archaeology

Scholarships for Graduate Students Academic Year 2021/2022

The scholarships are available for graduate students pursuing their M.A. or Ph.D. and who are registered as such in the Department of Archaeology and Near Eastern Studies at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. Scholarships are awarded on the basis of academic merit.

For details, press here.

 

Application deadline: June 5, 2021

 The submission is made by approval of the submitter at the end

 

Application Procedure

A. Please fill out the online application form.

B. Upload the required documents as single files, following these instructions.

  • Curriculum vitae

C. Please submit a recommendation written by your thesis supervisor; if you do not yet have a supervisor, submit a recommendation by one of your teachers. Recommendations must be uploaded according to these instructions.

D. At the end of the application procedure, please access the ‘application status’ page and act according to the instructions there.
