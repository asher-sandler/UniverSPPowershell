The Roger and Susan Hertog Center for the Archaeological Study of Jerusalem and Judah-2022

The Hertog Center has made M.A., Ph.D. and Post Doc scholarships available for excellent students who will pursue in the Institute of Archaeology at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, cutting-edge research on a range of topics related to the archaeology of Jerusalem and Judah throughout the ages.

The M.A. scholarship recipient will be awarded $20,000 per year for two years (October 2021–September 2023).

The Ph.D. scholarship recipient will be awarded $27,00 per year for up to four years (beginning October 2021).

The Postdoctoral scholarship recipient will be awarded $40,500 per year for one year (October 2021–September 2022) and is open to scholars who have received or have approval of their Ph.D. within the last three years before the scholarship commences.

 

Application deadline:  15.4.2021

The submission is made by the submitter’s approval at the end


Application procedure

1. Fill out the online application form.

2. Upload the following documents to the Personal Document Upload File according to these instructions.

For M.A. scholarships

  • Curriculum vitae

  • B.A. transcripts (and 1st year M.A. if relevant)

  • Short description of the intended subject of research (1 page)

  • Declaration of additional funding received for the M.A.

For Ph.D scholarships 

  • Curriculum vitae and List of Publications

  • M.A. transcripts

  • A short description on the intended subject of research (2 pages)

  • Declaration of additional funding received for the Ph.D

For Postdoctoral scholarships 

  • Curriculum vitae and List of Publications

  • A short description of the Ph.D thesis (up to 5 pages)

  • A short description of the intended subject of research and work plan (2 pages)

  • A letter of invitation from a staff member at the Institute

  • Declaration of additional funding received for the post-Doc

3. Two letters of recommendations, (for MA and PhD candidates: one of them from a potential supervisor - a member of the teaching staff at the Institute), are to be submitted according to this procedure.

4. To complete the process, please follow the instructions in the Application Submittal Status. 

 

For rules and regulations please see http://archaeology.huji.ac.il
