The Eliyahu Teomim Scholarships

Donated by ADAMA – 2020-2021

ADAMA company will award again this year four scholarships to outstanding graduate and graduate students whose studies and research focus on weed control.
The scholarship is for ~10,000 NIS.

The candidates will be selected and rated by the Faculty Scholarship Committee together with ADAMA Agricultural Solutions according to academic criteria.

Application deadline: February 1st, 2021

Application procedure

1.    Fill out the online application form.

2.    Upload the following documents (in separate files) to the Personal Document Upload File according to Documents Upload page 


First-year MSc students (outstanding students whose BSc point average is no less than 85 may apply for the scholarship provided that their thesis concerns weeds and weed control):

►   CV

►   Thesis topic and abstract (up to one page long)

►   Confirmation of enrollment for 2020-2021

►   BSc final record of studies

►   Letter explaining why the candidate is an appropriate choice for the Teomim scholarship

 

Second-year MSc students need to also include, in addition:

►   List of publications

►   Intermediate MSc record of studies

►   Research plan, approved by the supervisor

 

PhD students (outstanding students who began their studies after February 2019):

►   CV

►   List of publications

►   Thesis topic and abstract (up to one page)

►   Confirmation of enrollment for 2020-2021

►   Confirmation of enrollment to PhD (from the Authority for Research Students)

►   Final record of studies - BSc

►   Final record of studies - MSc

►   Intermediate record of studies - PhD

►   Letter explaining why the candidate is an appropriate choice for the Teomim scholarship

 

3.    A letter of recommendation from the supervisor (which also shows the relevance of the candidate's thesis to the research areas of the scholarship) is to be sent in accordance with  these procedures. 

4.    To complete the process, please follow the instructions in the Application Submittal Status.
