the Vidal Angel Post-Doctoral Fellowship for Research against Hatred and Bigotry

for the 2021-2022 Academic Year

 

The Fellowship includes:

  • Status of Research Fellow at the Hebrew University's Minerva Center for Human Rights for the 2021-2022 academic year
  • One-year scholarship in the amount of NIS 90,000
  • Funding for participation in international conferences

Requirements:

  • The Fellowship is open to researchers who received their Doctorate in any discipline related to human rights no earlier than October 2016, or who will submit their dissertation by May 2021
  • The research plan must be related to the fight against hatred, bigotry and intolerance
  • Commitment to complete an academic article or significant research project during the Fellowship year, and to present it at an academic forum organized by the Minerva Center, if so requested
  • Presence of at least 3 days a week at the Minerva Center for Human Rights at the Hebrew University, and involvement in the Center's ongoing academic activities, including assistance in organizing academic events
  • The Fellowship period: October 2021 – September 2022.


Deadline for applications:  March 31, 2021


Application Submission

A. Please fill out the Online Application form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:
  • Research plan (up to 2 pp., 1.5 spaced) describing the topics the applicant intends to research during the Fellowship year, methodology and relevance to the fight against hatred, bigotry and intolerance
  • Summary of the applicant's doctoral thesis (up to 2 pp. 1.5 spaced)
  • Curriculum vitae, including publications 
C. At least 2 recommendation letters (including one from the applicant's doctoral supervisor)
 - sent according to the guidelines described here

D. Please follow the instructions shown on the 'Submission Status' page
