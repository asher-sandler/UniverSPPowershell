The Hebrew University and The Lady Davis Fellowship Trust
For Visiting Professors - 2022/23

 

*PLEASE NOTE: One of the main aims of setting up an online system for applications is that each candidate can check that all the information he/she submitted has been uploaded.  We will not check the status of candidates and their application files.  IT IS THE CANDIDATES’ RESPONSIBILITY TO DO SO. 

 

How to Apply:

Please ensure that you fulfill the minimum criteria for eligibility for a Visiting Professorship as described HERE

If you do not satisfy the criteria the Trust will not be able to process your application.

 

Last date to submit applications: January 3nd 2022

 If you want to be safe that your file is complete, get your material in by the 29th of November…don't wait until the last minute.  We are not responsible for documents that didn't make it into your file after the deadline.

Submission Procedure

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Upload the following documents (in English OR Hebrew) according to the upload instructions:

  • Personal Letter - optional

  • Curriculum Vitae (Please do not include List of Publications in your CV)

  • A List of your Major Publications: This can include books you have edited or written chapters for, articles in non-refereed journals, editorials, abstracts or reviews. You may also include a list of  grants and contracts both extramural and intramural, consultant appointments, editorial board appointments, lectures at conferences, and whatever else you consider indicative of your academic achievements. (Example: Books, and Articles in Refereed Journals)

  • Proposed Activities (up to 2 pages): Please describe how you plan to spend the time under the Fellowship i.e. courses/seminars you propose to teach/conduct, and at which level (graduate or undergraduate), and/or research you propose to pursue.

  • A letter from your Main Sponsor: Expressing the interest of the Faculty/Department at the Hebrew University in the research you are pursuing - The academic main sponsors should transfer their letter/s directly to the applicant, the applicant should upload the letter to the system independently.

  • A Letter from Secondary Sponsors (optional): Expressing further support for your application - The academic secondary sponsors should transfer their letter/s directly to the applicant, the applicant should upload the letter to the system independently.

C. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
