The Department of Psychology

Tenure Track Faculty Positions - 2023-2024

Clinical Program

 

The Department of Psychology at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel: has an open rank tenure-track faculty opening in the clinical program: Applicants with a strong record of clinical research and training, consistent with scientist-practitioner, Boulder model required. Experience in psychotherapy research and theory preferred, but all areas are acceptable. Licensed or license-eligible in clinical psychology preferred. 

As a core faculty member in the well established clinical track of our nationally renowned program, responsibilities include maintaining an active research program, teaching both undergraduate (BA) and graduate (MA, PhD) classes in the department, and supervision of students' research.  Collaboration with other department members is encouraged. The Department of Psychology at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem is at the forefront of psychological research in Israel. The Hebrew University provides a strong foundation of resources to support all faculty members' research programs. It is located on Mount Scopus in Jerusalem, with approximately 20,000 students. 

We expect the position to start in the academic year of 2023-2024. Opening the position is subject to budgetary considerations. Submit a letter detailing current research and teaching interests, a curriculum vitae, representative reprints or preprints of publications, and three letters of reference by Sept 30, 2022.

For information about the department of psychology at The Hebrew University see https://en.psychology.huji.ac.il/. 

 

For further information about the positions, contact Iftah Yovel,
head of the clinical program (iftah.yovel@mail.huji.ac.il) or psych.search@mail.huji.ac.il


Applications should arrive by September 30th, 2022

 

Application Submission:

 

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the Documents Upload page:

  • Letter of application

  • Curriculum vitae

  • Statement of research

  • Statement of teaching interests

  • Up to 3 samples of representative reprints and preprints

C. Three letters of reference - sent according to the guidelines described here
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submissi​on Status' page
