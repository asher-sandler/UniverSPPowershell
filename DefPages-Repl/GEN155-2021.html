The Hebrew University and The Lady Davis Fellowship Trust
for One Year Extension to a Post-Doctoral Fellowship 2021/22

 
If this is the first time you are applying for a Post-Doctoral Fellowship with us you are in the WRONG place –
please Cancel Candidacy and apply for "The Hebrew University and The Lady Davis Fellowship Trust for Post-Doctoral Researchers 2021-22​"
 

*PLEASE NOTE:  One of the main aims of setting up an online system for applications is that each candidate can check that all the information he/she submitted has been uploaded.  We will not check the status of candidates and their application files.  IT IS THE CANDIDATES’ RESPONSIBILITY TO DO SO.

  

Submission Procedure

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Upload the following documents (in English OR Hebrew) according to the upload instructions:

  • Curriculum Vitae (Please do not include List of Publications in your CV)

  • List of Publications: This can include books you have edited or written chapters for, articles in non-refereed journals, editorials, abstracts or reviews. You may also include a list of  grants and contracts both extramural and intramural, consultant appointments, editorial board appointments, lectures at conferences, and whatever else you consider indicative of your academic achievements. (Example: Books, and Articles in Refereed Journals)

  • A letter requesting one extra year from the Post-Doc candidate

  • A letter requesting one extra year from your Sponsor - The academic main/secondary sponsors should transfer their letter/s directly to the applicant, the applicant should upload the letter to the system independently

C. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
 

Last date to submit applications: December 31st, 2020

  If you want to be safe that your file is complete, get your material in by the 30th of December…don't wait until the last minute.  We are not responsible for documents that didn't make it into your file after the deadline.
