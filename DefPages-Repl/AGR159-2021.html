Ariane de Rothschild Woman Doctoral program 2021-22

 

The Ariane de Rothschild Women's Doctoral Program grants scholarships to outstanding female PhD students from various fields of study, who study at research institutions in Israel. As part of the Baroness's efforts to promote equal opportunity for women around the world, the program supports talented and motivated female doctoral students from diverse backgrounds, whose scholarship is a necessary condition for their success in academia.

 

Eligibility

Excellent female PhD students who are Israeli residents and have an Israeli citizenship, registered for doctoral studies in current academic year 2020-2021 (no earlier than October 2020).

Screening includes examining candidates' compliance with the criteria of academic excellence (50%), and a personal background that takes into consideration social diversity, leadership, social involvement, and socio-economic needs (50%).

Scholarship information

The scholarship, 60,000 NIS is provided for up to four years, and is contingent upon the student's progress in studies and research.

The Hebrew University will match the scholarship and provide an additional sum of 40,000 NIS per year and 160,000 NIS in total for the four years of the program.

Students who receive the scholarships are committed to 56 hours of volunteering in educational projects each year, thus empowering young people and serving as role models, excellence and giving to the community.

Submission deadline: August 15, 2021

Application process

a.      Complete the online application form

b.      Upload the following documents (in PDF, one file for each document) to the personal documents folder according to these guidelines

  • CV (according to the guidelines)

  • List of publications, if applicable (according to the guidelines)

  • Grade transcripts (Higher education only, in English)

  • Research summary (1-2 pages)

  • Commitment form – page 3 of Call for Nominations – original document must be signed and uploaded

  • Letter from the candidate explaining his/her compatibility

c.      Send two letter of recommendation according to these guidelines

d.      To complete the process go to the Submission Status page and follow the guidelines

Please make sure to complete the above instructions entirely and precisely.
Files including incomplete forms or missing required documents will not be reviewed
