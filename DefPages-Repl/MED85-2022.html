International Master of Public Health Program - 2021/2022

 

Application Submission

A. Please complete the Online Application Form

B. The application must include the following documentation - according to the upload instructions (Do not upload zip files):

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)

  • Copies of relevant academic degrees (photocopy of diplomas)

  • Full academic transcripts of all previous university studies including separate transcripts for transfer credits (If transcripts are not in English, submit official translations and Copies of originals).          

  • Certificate of proficiency in English (TOEFL or IELTS)

  • Medical certificate form (HERE)

  • Photocopy of valid passport (Israeli Citizens – include Israeli ID and Passport)

  • Personal Statement*

  • Details concerning the job you will hold upon completion of MPH studies (if known) - according to template (download)

  • Scholarship request justification - A limited number of scholarships are available for outstanding and deserving IMPH applicants From low income and transition economy countries... To apply for scholarship support, justify your request in more than 300 words.

 
C. 3 letters of recommendation - sent according to this mechanism

D. Please follow the instructions that appear on 'Submission Status' page
 
PLEASE REVIEW THE ABOVE CAREFULLY AND ENSURE THAT ALL THE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS ARE UPLOADED
 

Deadline for submitting applications June 15, 2021

Decisions will be announced near the end of July.


Personal Statement*
Please write an autobiography (maximum 2 pages double spaced) in which you relate specifically to the following points:

  1. Your professional experiences and training prior to the job in which you are currently employed.

  2. Your reasons for wanting to undertake studies for the MPH degree.

  3. The reasons why you believe you qualify for this program.

  4. The job you expect to hold upon completion of MPH studies.

  5. The ways and the settings in which you feel you will be able to implement what you learn in this course in your country in the future.

  6. ​Any additional information relevant to your application.
