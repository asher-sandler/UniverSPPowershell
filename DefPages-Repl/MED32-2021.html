The School of Occupational Therapy at the Faculty of Medicine of The Hebrew University of Jerusalem is accepting applications for Tenure Track Position 2020-2021

 

 

The School of Occupational Therapy at the Faculty of Medicine of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem is accepting applications for tenure track.

Applicants should hold a PhD degree from a formal academic program in the field of Occupational Therapy, Public Health, Cognitive Science, Psychology, Etc. Priority will be given to candidates with Graduate Degree in Occupational Therapy (B.O.T.).

The Faculty will consider applicants that have demonstrated excellence and creativity during their doctoral and postdoctoral training. Successful applicants are expected to carry out independent competitive research programs and participate in undergraduate and graduate teaching.

The evaluation of the applicants/candidates for the academic position is based on innovation and excellence in research, commitment to mentoring and teaching and mainly on the applicant's potential to lead an original and breakthrough research in the field of Occupational Therapy.

We are looking for candidates that have a profound knowledge, broad view, and proven achievements in research, which can build and lead an independent research group and are committed to tutor students in all levels within the study programs of the School of Occupational Therapy.

 

Applicants should submit the following documents no later than March 22, 2020

Submission Procedure

A. Please fill out the Online Application form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Curriculum Vitae - please follow the instruction here

  • Cover letter / Applicants'  statement addressed to the dean, Prof. Dina Ben-Yehuda

  • List of Publications - please follow the instructions here

  • Passport picture

  • Scientific biography - description of your scientific achievements (one page)

  • Research plans  (no longer than three pages)

  • Teaching statement (not longer than one page)

 

C. Recommendation letters from three scientists who  are acquainted with the applicant and/or his/her research work (including previous and current mentors) - These letters should be sent as detailed here
 
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page

The first screening will be held in the Faculty's Search Committee. The Faculty's Search Committee will select a short list of applicants that will be invited for an interview at the Faculty.

 

For further details or questions please contact:

For Academic Information: Prof. Naomi Weintraub, Head of the School of Occupational Therapy | Email: naomi.weintraub@mail.huji.ac.il

For Administrative Assistance: Mrs. Kathia Shavit, Dean's Office | Email: kathias@savion.huji.ac.il

 

Submission deadline: March 22, 2020
