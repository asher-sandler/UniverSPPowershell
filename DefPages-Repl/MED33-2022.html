The School of Pharmacy at the Faculty of Medicine, The Hebrew University of Jerusalem - Tenure Track positions


The School of Pharmacy at the Faculty of Medicine of the Hebrew University of Jerusalem is accepting applications for tenure track academic positions for 2022-2023 academic year. 

Applicants should hold a PhD degree in science. Priority will be given to candidates with specialization in the areas of Medicinal Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Nanotechnology or Pharmacology. 

The evaluation of the applicants/candidates for the academic position is based on innovation and excellence in drug related research, commitment to mentoring and teaching and mainly on the applicant's potential to lead an original and breakthrough research.

We are looking for candidates that have a profound knowledge, broad view, and proven achievements in research, which can build and lead an independent research group and are committed to tutor students in all levels within the study programs of the School of Pharmacy (B. Pharm, MSc. and PhD).

Applicants should submit the following documents no later than Monday, October 4th, 2021.  

 

Submission Procedure

A. Please fill out the Online Application form

B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Curriculum Vitae - please follow the instruction here  (CV template)
  • Cover letter / Applicants'  statement addressed to the dean, Prof. Dina Ben-Yehuda
  • List of Publications - please follow the instructions here
  • Passport picture
  • Scientific biography - description of your scientific achievements (one page)
  • Research plans  (no longer than three pages)
  • Teaching statement (not longer than one page)

C. Recommendation letters from three scientists who  are acquainted with the applicant and/or his/her research work (including previous and current mentors) - These letters should be sent as detailed here

D.  Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page

 

The first screening will be held in the School of Pharmacy and in the Faculty's Search Committee.

The Faculty's Search Committee will select a short list of applicants that will be invited for an interview at the Faculty.

 

For further details please contact:

For Academic Information: Prof. Boaz Tirosh, Head of the School of Pharmacy

Email: boazt@ekmd.huji.ac.il  

For Administrative Assistance: Mrs. Kathia Shavit, Dean's Office: kathias@savion.huji.ac.il

 

 

Submission deadline: Monday, October 4, 2021
