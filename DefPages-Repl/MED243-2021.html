Sussman Foundation Postdoc Fellowship 2021-22

General Information

 

The Sussman Foundation is offering two one-year $20,000 fellowships to Postdoctoral students who will begin their Postdoc term in 2021-22 in elite universities in the USA, and who are in need of financial support.

The fellowship may be extended for a second year.

 

Submission deadline: June 27, 2021

Application process

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Upload all the following documents, in English, to your personal documents folder according to Documents Upload page :
    • CV Including: Education, Work experience, Teaching experience, Research experience, Awards, Scientific conferences
    • List of publications according to template
    • Research abstract one page including: Research field, Project title, Abstract
    • A letter of appointment from the institution in which you will be conducting your postdoctoral research
    • Personal story write a letter to the foundation in which you tell about yourself (about one and a half pages). Please include:
      • A description of your financial situation (specifically, do you have any other sources of funding?)
      • A written description of yourself, your background and family, what motivated you to perform research in your field and a bit about your journey in life and academia

 

  1. Two or three letters of recommendation (including one from your PhD instructor) should be uploaded according to Documents Upload page
  2. To complete the submission process go to the Submission Status page and follow the guidelines

Important: Please request that the letters of recommendation written on your behalf include the person's letterhead and electronic signature

Please make sure to complete the above instructions entirely and precisely. Files including incomplete forms or missing required documents will not be reviewed
