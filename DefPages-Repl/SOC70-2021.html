The Department of Psychology

Human Psychobiology  - Tenure Track Faculty Positions - 2021

 

The Department of Psychology and the Department of Cognitive Sciences at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel have an open rank joint tenure-track faculty opening in human-psychobiology. We are seeking applicants who integrate biology and psychology and seek to uncover the biological mechanisms that underlie psychological processes in humans.

Responsibilities include maintaining an active research program, teaching both undergraduate (BA) and graduate (MA, PhD) classes in the department, and supervision of students' research. Collaboration with other department members is encouraged.

The Departments of Psychology and Cognitive Sciences at The Hebrew University of Jerusalem are at the forefront of psychological research in Israel. The Hebrew University provides a strong foundation of resources to support all faculty members' research programs. It is located at Mount Scopus in Jerusalem, with approximately 20,000 students. 

We expect the position to start in the academic year of 2021-2022. Opening the position is subject to budgetary considerations. Please submit a letter detailing current research and teaching interests, a curriculum vitae, representative reprints or preprints of publications, and three letters of reference by Octoberr 15th, 2020.

For information about the Department of Psychology at The Hebrew University see https://en.psychology.huji.ac.il/. 

For further information about the position, contact Maya Tamir, Chair of the Department of Psychology (tamirm@mail.huji.ac.il) or Hilla Jacobson, Chair of the Department of Cognitive Sciences (hilla.jacobson@mail.huji.ac.il).

Applications should arrive by October 31th, 2020

 

Application Submission:

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Letter of application

  • Curriculum vitae

  • Statement of research

  • Statement of teaching interests

  • Up to 3 samples of representative reprints and preprints

C. Three letters of reference - sent according to the guidelines described here
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submissi​on Status' page
