Open academic position at

The Faculty of Medicine at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem

 

The Faculty of Medicine at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem wishes to recruit a virologist at a level of assistant or associate professor (tenure track) or full professor (tenure), for the 2022-2023 academic year.

Considering the great challenges of viral epidemics, the Hebrew University and Faculty of Medicine decided to give virology research a high priority and built a modern infrastructure for virus research, including a State-of-the-art high biosafety facility for cell and animal research (BSL3 and ABSL3 laboratories). The appointed faculty member is expected to serve as the director of this new facility. In addition, she/he will be provided with laboratory space and a competitive start-up package.

Candidates holding MD, PhD or both degrees who run highly competitive research programs in the field of virology are encouraged to apply. Physician-researchers, who wish to have a combined clinical and laboratory research career are also very welcome.

We are looking for candidates that have a profound knowledge, broad view, and proven achievements in virology research, which can build and lead an independent research group and are committed to mentor students at all levels within the learning programs of the School of Medicine.

 

Applicants should submit the following documents no later than April 27, 2022

 

Submission Procedure:

A. Please fill out the Online Application form

B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Curriculum Vitae - please follow the instruction here (CV template)

  • Cover letter / Applicant's  statement addressed to the dean, Prof. Dina Ben-Yehuda

  • List of Publications - please follow the instructions here (List of Publications Template)

  • Passport picture

  • Scientific biography - description of your scientific achievements (one page)

  • Research plans  (up to three pages)

  • Teaching statement (one page)

C. Recommendation letters from three scientists who  are acquainted with the applicant and/or his/her research work (including previous and current mentors) - These letters should be sent as detailed here

D.  Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page

 

The Faculty's Search Committee will select a short list of applicants that will be invited for an interview at the Faculty.

For further details please contact:

For academic information: Prof. Eli Pikarsky, head of the faculty's search committee: peli@hadassah.org.il

For administrative assistance: Mrs. Kathia Shavit, dean's office: kathias@savion.huji.ac.il

 

 Submission deadline April, 27 2022
