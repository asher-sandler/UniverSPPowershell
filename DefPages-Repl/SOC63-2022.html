The Department of Sociology and Anthropology

Tenure Track Faculty Positions - 2021

 

The Department of Sociology and Anthropology seeks to fill a tenure track position beginning October 2021.The position is open to highly qualified sociologists, anthropologists and demographers with a strong record of publications.  Proffered eras of specialization are qualitative methods, social stratification, immigration, labor market, economic sociology and different fields in cultural anthropology. 

The ideal candidate will have a clear and substantial scholarly agenda and demonstrate an ability to publish innovative and high-quality research in top-tier journals or books with notable publishing houses. Applicants with insufficient publications may be encouraged to defer their candidacy and apply for a post-doctoral fellowship with the department (http://ldft.huji.ac.il/; http://buberfellows.huji.ac.il/).  

Applicants should also note that while we, like other social science departments at our university, invite international scholars to join our faculty, the principal, even if not mandatory, language of instruction remains Hebrew.

Applications should be submitted as soon as possible and no later than September 30, 2020. We expect to notify candidates if they have the endorsement of the department by late January 2021. Final university approval of appointments is expected by March 2021.

For additional information about our department and this procedure, please contact the department chair, Prof. Michal Frenkel, at michal.frenkel1@mail.huji.ac.il and visit our webpage: http://sociology.huji.ac.il.

 

Deadline for application is October 10th, 2020

 

Application Submission

A. Please fill out the Online Application Form
 
B. Please upload the following documents according to the upload instructions:

  • Curriculum Vitae

  • A Statement of Academic biography

  • Three published or ready-for-publication items

 
C. At least 3 letters of recommendation - sent according to the guidelines described here
 
D. Please follow the instructions shown on 'Submission Status' page
